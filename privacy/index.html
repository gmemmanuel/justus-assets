Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Justus Health ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Justus app.

1. What We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account: Email address when you create an account.
  • Profile: Name, date of birth, gender, height, and weight. All optional - you choose what to share.
  • Fitness profile: Goals, experience level, equipment access, schedule availability, activity preferences, and injury history. Used to personalize your coaching.
  • Conversations: Messages you send to your AI coach, including text and voice input transcriptions.

Health and fitness data

With your explicit permission, we import data from Apple HealthKit (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android), including:

  • Activity and workout data
  • Daily summaries (steps, calories, resting heart rate)
  • Sleep data
  • Body measurements

You grant this access through the iOS or Android permission prompt. You can revoke access at any time from your device settings or within the app.

Data collected automatically

  • Usage events: Behavioral metadata such as when you generate a plan, log a workout, or complete onboarding. We use this to understand how the app is used and to improve it. See Section 3.
  • Crash and error data: Technical information about app errors and crashes. This does not include your conversation content or health data.
  • Device and app version: Used for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

To provide personalized coaching

Your profile, conversation history, wearable data, and workout logs are used together to generate training plans and coaching responses. Keeping this history is what allows the coaching to be relevant and adaptive over time.

To improve the app

We review de-identified usage data - including patterns in conversation interactions, coaching responses, and app behavior - to improve the quality, safety, and effectiveness of the coaching experience. Direct identifiers (name, email) are removed before any internal review. We do not sell this data or share it with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Safety monitoring

We log certain safety events - for example, when a potential medical concern is detected in a conversation - for liability protection and product safety review. See Section 7 for retention rules.

Product analytics

We use PostHog to understand aggregate behavior across users (for example, activation rates and retention). PostHog receives behavioral metadata only - it does not receive your conversation content, health metrics, or profile information.

Crash reporting

We use Sentry to detect and fix technical errors. Crash reports include device and app state but are scrubbed of your name, email, and conversation content before transmission.

3. Analytics and Product Improvement

We use your data to improve the Justus coaching experience. This means:

  • Behavioral analytics (PostHog): Aggregated, anonymized events about how users interact with the app. No conversation content or health data is sent to PostHog.
  • Internal review: We may review de-identified conversation patterns and coaching interactions internally to evaluate coaching quality and identify improvements. This analysis uses data with direct identifiers removed. It stays internal - it is not shared with third parties and is not used for advertising.
  • LLM monitoring (Portkey): We track request metadata (token counts, latency, error rates) through Portkey to monitor AI quality and cost. Portkey does not receive conversation content.

We will never sell your data. We will never share your data with advertisers or use it to target you with ads.

5. AI Models and Training Data

We do not use your personal data to train AI models ourselves.

For the AI providers that receive your conversation content:

Google (Gemini via Vertex AI): Your conversations are processed via Google Cloud's Vertex AI platform. Google's Vertex AI terms state that customer data is not used to train or improve Google's foundation models. Google Cloud is covered under Google's HIPAA Business Associate Agreement.

OpenAI: OpenAI's standard API does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models by default, per their usage policies.

Deepgram: Audio submitted via the Deepgram API is used only to produce a transcript and is not retained for training purposes under their API terms.

Portkey: Portkey routes requests between the app and model providers. It processes request metadata but does not retain conversation content.

6. Health Data

Health data imported from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect is used exclusively to provide you with personalized coaching.

We do not:

  • Use your health data for advertising or marketing
  • Share your health data with third parties for any purpose other than delivering your coaching (see the provider table in Section 4)
  • Sell your health data

This is consistent with Apple's HealthKit developer guidelines and Google's Health Connect developer policy.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.

When you delete your account, we permanently delete your profile, conversation history, health data, training plans, and workout logs.

Exception - safety records: Certain safety and compliance records (such as a log that a medical concern was detected during a coaching session) are retained after account deletion for liability and safety audit purposes. These records contain minimal information (event type, timestamp, and general reason) - they do not contain your full conversation history, profile, or personal identifiers beyond your account ID.

8. Your Rights

  • Access and export: You can download a copy of all your data in JSON format from within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app. Deletion is permanent and takes effect within 30 days.
  • Wearable data: You can disconnect HealthKit or Health Connect at any time from app settings. Imported wearable data will be deleted upon request.
  • Questions or requests: Contact us at coachjonathanapp@gmail.com.

9. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • We use row-level security so users can only access their own data.
  • API keys and secrets are never stored on your device.

10. Children's Privacy

Justus is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email before changes take effect. Continued use of the app after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
